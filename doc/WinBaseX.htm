Not included in this are:

  • XShape routines for non-rectangular windows
  • Xcms routines for color management
  • Xdbe routines from the double buffering extension
  • Xmb routines for multibyte character sets
  • Xwc routines for UNICODE characters
  • Xmbuf routines for the multibuffer and stereo extensions

Routines outside the scope of this design effort:

  • Xt routines (Widgets)
  • Xm, Exm, Mrm, Xme, XmVa routines (Motif)
  • XF86DGA, XF86Misc, and XF86Vid routines for XFree86 extensions
  • Xau routines for client/server authentication
